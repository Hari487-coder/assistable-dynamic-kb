Your AI agent, with live data.

Live KB is a knowledge base you own, that your Assistable voice and chat agents query in real time — inventory, products, prices, availability. Free to run. Three clicks to set up. Your data never touches anyone else's server.

2

Open your new Live KB and create your account

When Render finishes (~2 minutes), open your instance's URL. The first account created becomes the owner and sign-ups close automatically — it's yours alone.

3

Follow the in-app setup

The setup page walks you through the rest: paste your Assistable API key, add your data (CSV, feed, website, or database), tick which assistants get the tool — it's created in your Assistable account automatically — then paste one paragraph into your assistant's prompt and test it live on the same page.

Free-tier note: Render's free plan sleeps after 15 idle minutes and its disk resets on redeploys — great for trying it out. For real customer traffic, the always-free VM option (one command, permanent disk) is the recommended $0 production home.

What accounts do I need?

Just two: Render (free, hosts your instance) and your existing Assistable account (for the API key). The VM option swaps Render for Oracle Cloud.

Does it work on phone calls?

Yes — the same tool serves voice and chat automatically. Answers come back in well under a second with a ready-to-speak summary.

What data can I connect?

CSV uploads, feed URLs (JSON/CSV/XML — every DMS and store platform can export one), whole websites, and read-only Postgres/Supabase.

What does it cost?

Nothing. No paid APIs, no paid database, free hosting tiers. The design goal was $0 to run — which also made it fast: lookups are ~30-60ms.
